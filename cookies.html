Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2025

This page explains how the Neatmark client intake form uses cookies and browser storage. We keep this minimal. We are not an advertising platform, we do not track you across the web, and we do not sell or share browsing data with anyone.

What are cookies

Cookies are small pieces of data stored in your browser by a website you visit. Some are essential for a site to function, and some are used to remember your preferences. Our form uses browser local storage rather than traditional cookies for most of its functionality, but the rules are the same and you deserve to know about both.

What we store and why

The table below covers everything the Neatmark form stores in your browser.

Name Type Purpose Expires
user-theme Local storage Remembers your preferred color theme (light, dark, or auto) so it is applied on every visit without asking again. Until you clear browser data
brand-intake-draft Local storage Auto-saves your form answers as you type so you do not lose progress if you close the tab or navigate away. Cleared when you submit or click Clear Draft. Until submission or manual clear
preferred-language Local storage Saves your language selection so the form loads in the right language on your next visit. Until you clear browser data

None of these items are used for tracking, analytics, or advertising. They exist purely to make the form more comfortable to use.

Third-party services

Our form uses a small number of external resources to function properly:

  • Google Fonts loads the typefaces used in the form design. When your browser requests a font, Google may log your IP address as part of the standard web request. We do not control this. You can read Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com.
  • Netlify Identity handles administrator authentication for the dashboard. It sets its own session cookies to keep admins logged in. This only applies to Neatmark staff, not to clients filling out the form.

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other advertising or behavioral tracking tool. No third-party marketing service receives any data about you or your visit.

How to clear or disable storage

You are in full control of what gets stored in your browser. You can clear any of the items listed above at any time by going to your browser's settings and clearing site data for this domain. Most browsers also let you block local storage entirely, though doing so may affect how the form saves your draft progress.

The form itself also includes a Clear Draft button that removes the saved draft from your browser without affecting any other stored preferences.

We do not use advertising cookies

We want to be clear about this. No advertising network tracks visits to this form. No retargeting pixel fires when you load this page. We do not use your visit to serve you ads elsewhere on the internet. The data you share here stays here, used only to work on your project with you.

Changes to this policy

If we ever add new storage items or third-party services, we will update this page and the date at the top. We will not introduce tracking or advertising technologies without being transparent about it here first.

Questions

If you have any questions about cookies or how your browser data is handled, you can reach us through our website at neatmark.studio.
